## Availability SLA

### Definition

**Availability** = (Total Minutes - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes × 100

### Exclusions

<!-- FILL: Document what doesn't count against SLA -->
- Scheduled maintenance (with proper notice)
- Force majeure events
- Third-party service outages (with documented dependency)
- Customer-caused issues
- Beta/preview features

## SLA Credits

### Credit Schedule (PRO/ENTERPRISE)

<!-- FILL: Document credit structure -->
| Availability | Credit |
|--------------|--------|
| 99.0% - 99.9% | 10% |
| 95.0% - 99.0% | 25% |
| < 95.0% | 50% |

### Credit Process

1. Customer opens support ticket within 30 days
2. Support verifies SLA breach
3. Credit applied to next invoice
4. Maximum credit: 50% of monthly fee

### Credit Exclusions

- FREE tier (no SLA)
- Breaches caused by customer
- Scheduled maintenance
- Force majeure
