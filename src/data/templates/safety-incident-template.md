## Purpose

Use this template to document AI safety incidents in multi-tenant platforms. Comprehensive incident documentation enables effective response, root cause analysis, prevention of recurrence, and organizational learning. In multi-tenant environments, safety incidents require additional analysis of tenant isolation, cross-tenant impact, and coordinated communication with affected tenants. This template ensures consistent incident handling aligned with responsible AI practices.
