## Next Steps

### On PASS
1. Proceed to module-level design (`create-module-architecture`)
2. Begin QG-M1 preparation
3. Archive master architecture (frozen)

### On CONDITIONAL
1. Execute mitigation plan
2. Schedule follow-up validation by deadline
3. Proceed with caution on non-blocked items

### On FAIL
1. Review critical failures
2. Execute recovery protocol
3. Schedule re-validation
4. Escalate if Attempt 2 fails
