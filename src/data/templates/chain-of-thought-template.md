## Purpose

Use this template to design chain-of-thought (CoT) prompts that encourage transparent, step-by-step reasoning. CoT prompting is critical for multi-tenant AI systems where decision auditability, explainability, and tenant-specific reasoning contexts are required. This template ensures reasoning chains remain within tenant boundaries while providing traceable decision paths for compliance and debugging.

## Chain-of-Thought Structure

### Reasoning Framework

```text
## Problem Analysis

Let me analyze this step by step within the context of tenant {{tenant_id}}.

### Step 1: Understand the Request
- What is being asked: {{request_summary}}
- Tenant context: {{tenant_context}}
- Constraints: {{constraints}}

### Step 2: Gather Relevant Information
- Available data sources (tenant-scoped): {{data_sources}}
- Applicable rules/policies: {{policies}}
- Historical context: {{history}}

### Step 3: Evaluate Options
For each potential approach:
- Option A: {{option_a}}
  - Pros: {{pros_a}}
  - Cons: {{cons_a}}
  - Tenant impact: {{impact_a}}

- Option B: {{option_b}}
  - Pros: {{pros_b}}
  - Cons: {{cons_b}}
  - Tenant impact: {{impact_b}}

### Step 4: Apply Reasoning
- Primary consideration: {{primary}}
- Secondary factors: {{secondary}}
- Tenant-specific factors: {{tenant_factors}}

### Step 5: Reach Conclusion
- Selected approach: {{conclusion}}
- Confidence level: {{confidence}}
- Reasoning summary: {{reasoning_summary}}

### Step 6: Validate Against Constraints
- [ ] Within tenant boundaries
- [ ] Complies with tier limitations
- [ ] Meets safety requirements
- [ ] Audit trail complete
```
