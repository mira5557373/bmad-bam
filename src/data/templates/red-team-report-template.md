## Purpose

Use this template to document AI red team findings from adversarial testing exercises. Red teaming is essential for identifying vulnerabilities in AI systems before malicious actors exploit them. In multi-tenant environments, red teaming must specifically test tenant isolation boundaries, cross-tenant attack vectors, and ensure that compromise of one tenant cannot affect others. This template captures attack methodologies, findings, risk assessments, and remediation guidance.
