## Purpose

Use this template to document comprehensive AI bias assessments for multi-tenant platforms. Bias assessment is critical for ensuring fair, equitable AI behavior across diverse tenant populations, complying with AI regulations (EU AI Act, NYC Local Law 144), and maintaining trust with users. This template covers demographic bias, representational bias, allocative bias, and tenant-specific bias considerations unique to multi-tenant architectures.
