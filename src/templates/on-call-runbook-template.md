## Alert Triage

### Severity Levels

| Severity | Description | Response | Example |
|----------|-------------|----------|---------|
| P1/Critical | Service down, all tenants | Immediate | Database unreachable |
| P2/High | Degraded, multiple tenants | 15 min | High latency |
| P3/Medium | Limited impact, single tenant | 1 hour | Feature broken |
| P4/Low | Minor issue, workaround exists | Next business day | UI glitch |

### Multi-Tenant Priority Matrix

| Tenant Tier | P1 Response | P2 Response | P3 Response |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| Enterprise | 5 min | 15 min | 1 hour |
| Pro | 15 min | 30 min | 4 hours |
| Free | 30 min | 1 hour | Next business day |

## Common Procedures

### Initial Response Checklist

- [ ] Acknowledge alert within SLA
- [ ] Check monitoring dashboards
- [ ] Identify affected tenants
- [ ] Start incident channel (if P1/P2)
- [ ] Begin investigation
- [ ] Update status page (if needed)

### Communication Templates

**Status Page Update (P1):**
```
[Investigating] We are currently investigating an issue affecting {service}.
Some users may experience {symptoms}. We will provide updates every 30 minutes.
```

**Tenant Communication (Enterprise):**
```
Subject: Service Incident Notification - {{project_name}}
We detected an issue affecting your service at {time}.
Impact: {description}
Current status: {status}
Next update: {time}
```
