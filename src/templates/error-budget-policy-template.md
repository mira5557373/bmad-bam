## Error Budget Calculation

### Monthly Budget by SLO

| SLO Target | Monthly Budget | Minutes/Month | Example |
|------------|----------------|---------------|---------|
| 99.9% | 0.1% | 43.2 min | Standard tier |
| 99.95% | 0.05% | 21.6 min | Enterprise tier |
| 99.99% | 0.01% | 4.32 min | Critical services |

### Budget Consumption Formula

```
Consumed Budget = (Bad Minutes / Total Minutes) * 100
Remaining Budget = Monthly Budget - Consumed Budget
Burn Rate = Consumed Budget / Days Elapsed * 30
```

## Policy Thresholds

### Green Zone (Budget > 50%)

| Activity | Status | Approval Required |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Feature releases | ✅ Allowed | Standard process |
| Risky deployments | ✅ Allowed | Team approval |
| Infrastructure changes | ✅ Allowed | Standard process |
| Experimentation | ✅ Allowed | Team approval |

### Yellow Zone (Budget 25-50%)

| Activity | Status | Approval Required |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Feature releases | ⚠️ Cautious | Tech lead approval |
| Risky deployments | ⚠️ Limited | Director approval |
| Infrastructure changes | ⚠️ Essential only | Tech lead approval |
| Experimentation | ❌ Paused | N/A |

### Red Zone (Budget < 25%)

| Activity | Status | Approval Required |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Feature releases | ❌ Frozen | VP approval only |
| Risky deployments | ❌ Frozen | VP approval only |
| Infrastructure changes | ❌ Reliability only | Director approval |
| Experimentation | ❌ Frozen | N/A |

### Budget Exhausted (Budget = 0%)

| Activity | Status | Duration |
|----------|--------|----------|
| All feature work | ❌ Stopped | Until budget recovers |
| On-call priority | Reliability | 100% focus |
| Team allocation | Reliability fixes | Full team |
