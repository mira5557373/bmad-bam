## SLO Definitions

### Availability SLO

| SLO ID | Target | Window | Measurement |
|--------|--------|--------|-------------|
| SLO-AVAIL-001 | 99.9% | 30 days rolling | (successful requests / total requests) * 100 |
| SLO-AVAIL-002 | 99.95% | 30 days rolling | Enterprise tier availability |

**Error Budget:**
- Monthly budget: 43.2 minutes downtime (99.9%)
- Enterprise budget: 21.6 minutes downtime (99.95%)

### Latency SLO

| SLO ID | Target | Percentile | Window |
|--------|--------|------------|--------|
| SLO-LAT-001 | < 200ms | p50 | 30 days rolling |
| SLO-LAT-002 | < 500ms | p95 | 30 days rolling |
| SLO-LAT-003 | < 1000ms | p99 | 30 days rolling |

## SLO Alerting Rules

### Alert Thresholds

| Alert | Condition | Severity | Action |
|-------|-----------|----------|--------|
| Error budget burn rate | > 2x normal | Warning | Notify on-call |
| Error budget burn rate | > 10x normal | Critical | Page on-call |
| Error budget exhausted | 0% remaining | Critical | Freeze deployments |
