## Purpose

Use this template to design few-shot prompts that demonstrate desired behavior through carefully selected examples. Few-shot prompting is essential for ensuring consistent, high-quality AI responses across tenant contexts while maintaining isolation and respecting tier-specific capabilities. This template provides structure for example selection, formatting, and evaluation.
