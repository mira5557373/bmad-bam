### Quota Alert Configuration

```yaml
quota_alerts:
  api_requests:
    metric: tenant.api_requests.count
    window: {{api_window}}
    thresholds:

        action: notify
      - level: critical
        percentage: {{api_critical}}
        action: notify
      - level: exhausted
        percentage: 100
        action: notify_and_throttle
```
