## Purpose

Use this template to document significant architecture decisions for multi-tenant AI platforms. Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) capture the context, drivers, options considered, and rationale for decisions. In multi-tenant environments, ADRs must explicitly address tenant isolation implications, tier-specific considerations, and cross-tenant impact. This template follows the MADR (Markdown Any Decision Records) format while adding multi-tenant-specific sections essential for SaaS platform documentation.
