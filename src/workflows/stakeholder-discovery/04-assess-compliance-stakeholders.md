# Step 04: Assess Compliance Stakeholders

## Purpose

Identify and assess all stakeholders responsible for compliance, security, privacy, and regulatory oversight within tenant organizations. These stakeholders are critical gatekeepers for AI platform adoption and require special attention due to their authority to block or delay implementations based on governance concerns.

## Prerequisites

- [ ] Steps 01-03 are complete
- [ ] Understanding of applicable regulatory frameworks (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2, FedRAMP, etc.)
- [ ] Knowledge of tenant industry sectors and their compliance requirements
- [ ] AI governance and ethics policy documentation (if available)
- [ ] Data classification and handling requirements

## Actions

1. **Identify Regulatory Frameworks by Tenant Type**
   - Map applicable regulations to tenant categories:
     - **Enterprise (General):** SOC2, ISO 27001, GDPR/CCPA
     - **Healthcare Tenants:** HIPAA, HITECH, FDA regulations
     - **Financial Services:** SOX, PCI-DSS, FINRA, Basel III
     - **Government:** FedRAMP, FISMA, NIST 800-53, StateRAMP
     - **International:** GDPR, regional data sovereignty laws
   - Document AI-specific regulations (EU AI Act, emerging state laws)

2. **Map Compliance Stakeholder Roles**
   - Identify key compliance positions:
     - Chief Compliance Officer (CCO)
     - Data Protection Officer (DPO)
     - Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
     - Privacy Officer/Manager
     - Internal Audit Lead
     - Legal Counsel (technology/data focus)
     - Risk Management Officer
   - Document their specific domains and authority levels

3. **Assess AI-Specific Governance Stakeholders**
   - Identify AI governance roles (may be emerging):
     - AI Ethics Officer/Committee
     - Responsible AI Lead
     - Model Risk Management Team
     - AI Audit Function
     - Algorithmic Fairness Reviewers
   - Document their concerns specific to AI platforms

4. **Document Compliance Review Requirements**
   - For each compliance stakeholder, identify:
     - Required review gates and checkpoints
     - Documentation requirements (assessments, certifications)
     - Approval authority and veto power
     - Typical review timelines
     - Escalation procedures

5. **Map Security Review Stakeholders**
   - Identify security-focused stakeholders:
     - Security Architecture Review Board
     - Penetration Testing Team
     - Vulnerability Management
     - Security Operations Center (SOC)
     - Third-Party Risk Management
   - Document their involvement in AI platform evaluations

6. **Identify External Compliance Stakeholders**
   - Map external parties with compliance influence:
     - Regulatory bodies and auditors
     - Industry certification bodies
     - External audit firms
     - Legal and compliance consultants
     - Insurance underwriters (cyber liability)

7. **Create Compliance Stakeholder Profiles**
   - Document for each compliance stakeholder:
     - Regulatory domains they oversee
     - Key concerns regarding AI platforms
     - Required evidence and documentation
     - Historical approval patterns
     - Preferred engagement approach

8. **Assess Compliance Blockers and Accelerators**
   - Identify potential compliance showstoppers
   - Document pre-existing certifications that accelerate approval
   - Map compliance stakeholder relationships and alliances
   - Note any compliance fast-track programs

## Verification

- [ ] All applicable regulatory frameworks are identified per tenant type
- [ ] Compliance stakeholders are mapped with authority levels
- [ ] AI-specific governance roles are documented
- [ ] Review requirements and timelines are captured
- [ ] Compliance stakeholder profiles are complete
- [ ] External compliance influencers are identified

## Outputs

| Output | Description | Format |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Regulatory Framework Matrix | Regulations mapped to tenant types | Spreadsheet |
| Compliance Stakeholder Registry | List of all compliance stakeholders | Spreadsheet |
| Compliance Review Requirements | Documentation and approval requirements | Document |
| Compliance Stakeholder Profiles | Detailed profiles of key compliance roles | Document |
| Compliance Risk Assessment | Potential blockers and accelerators | Document |
